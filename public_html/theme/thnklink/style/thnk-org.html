program

Learn by doing
& make it happen

Breaking with the limitations of traditional education, at THNK you will work on challenges projects on the most important topics in life – with personal coaching and our unique Accelerator. Because we believe the next generation of leaders must be creative. And have the curiosity, courage and power to develop new ideas and solutions that transform the way you think. And work.

people

Participants, Faculty, staff
& board

Among our founding team, we are proud to have amazing organizations and an international group of pioneering creative leaders. Including people from Stanford d.school, Droog Design, McKinsey & Company, Indian School of Business, Philips Design – as well as designer Marcel Wanders, UNStudio architect Ben van Berkel and educational maverick, Esther Wojcicki.
